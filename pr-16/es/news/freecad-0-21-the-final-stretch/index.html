FreeCAD 0.21 - The Final Stretch

22 de mayo de 2023 Chris Hennes (80)Announcement (94)Release (16)129 palabras1 min

We are just a few weeks from the release of FreeCAD 0.21, and we need your help to get across the finish line! Most critically, we need more folks to weigh in on the “No Status” issues and PRs in the GitHub project for 0.21. If you’re interested in helping with development, please take a look through this list and see if there’s something you can help with, either by testing, verifying bugs, or even developing a fix. If you want to help with development and need help getting started, take a look at the Developer’s Handbook. You can also ask for help on the Forum, Discord, Reddit, Mastodon, or any of the other social media sites linked to from our Homepage.
