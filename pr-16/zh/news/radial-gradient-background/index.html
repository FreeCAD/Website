Radial Gradient Background

2023年4月10日 Chris Hennes (80)Feature (18)UI (7)51 字1 分钟

For many years FreeCAD has supported a linear gradient background: at long last, developer @xtemp09 has answered the frequent call for a radial gradient. Coupled with @PaddleStroke’s rearrangement of the colors Preferences page, FreeCAD now sports an overhauled background option that is sure to make many folks quite happy. Enjoy!
