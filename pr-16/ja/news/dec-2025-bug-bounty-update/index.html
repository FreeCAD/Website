Dec 2025 Bug Bounty Update

2025年12月19日 Aleksandr Prokudin (169)Update (158)FPA (44)Bounty (4)68 語1 分

We are currently at 17 release blockers for FreeCAD 1.1, so the FPA members have raised the price per fixed release blocker from $50 to $100.

Meanwhile, two more people have collected bugfix rewards:

  • Werner Meyer, who is a co-founder of FreeCAD.
  • Chris “ipatch” Jones, who previously contributed numerous improvements benefiting Mac users.

For more information on the bugfix rewards program, please see our earlier blog post.
