FreeCAD at FOSDEM

2024年2月1日 Jo Hinchliffe (77)Announcement (94)FOSDEM (7)125 語1 分

This weekend in Brussels lots of members of the FreeCAD community will be at FOSDEM, the amazing free event for all things opensource. If you are attending FOSDEM please come and spend some time at the FreeCAD stand which will be in the K Building. We have some excellent neighbours, Pine64 and Prusa and we hope that FreeCAD sponsors Libre Space Foundation should also have a presence in this space.

As a reminder FOSDEM runs from 9 am on Saturday with it’s amazing program of speakers and talks. On Sunday FreeCAD core developer Yorik will be delivering a FreeCAD State of the Union address. If you want to check this out it will be at 10.15 in room H1308 (Rolin).
